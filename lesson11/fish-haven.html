Your fishing checklist: Enjoy Fish Haven to the max

Fish Haven has a lot to offer, especially when it comes to the main attraction: The fishing. We've put together this list to help you ensure you are ready for the best fishing excursion!

  • freshwater or fly fishing rod
  • Fishing net (especially if you want to catch some Bonneville Cisco!)
  • small-large bait and tackle.
  • sun protection (hat, sunglasses, SPF 30 sunscreen or higher)
  • 5 gallon bucket for holding catches
  • tape measure for measuring length of catches
