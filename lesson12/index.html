About Us

The Neptune City Chamber of Commerce, or NC3 is here to help support local businesses

There is nothing quite like the feeling of having good neighbors. Our vision is that Neptune City be like one big happy family, neighborly, and supportive of each other. The purpose of the NC3 is to provide this atmosphere in a way that supports local businesses through service and events that everyone can enjoy.

Goals

  • Provide up-to-date info on local events. Be in the know so you can spend some quality time here in your neighborhood!
  • Spotlight local businesses who work hard to give you the best services in Neptune City
  • Support the bond between local business owners and the community. We're all neighbors, let's support each other.
