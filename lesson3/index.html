Site Plan

Proposed site name: Meteorology Station

Site Purpose: Help aspiring meteorologists connect with peers and learn meteorology together. The Meteorology Station is a place where future weather forecasters and meteorologists can hone their skills and collaborate with peers. The site will be location focused, so meteorologists in a given area can collaborate with others in the area. Weather forecasting, news, learning forum, and more.
